A Smarter Workflow from Slide to Report

Our platform integrates seamlessly into your lab, providing powerful tools at every step of the diagnostic process.

From Pixels to Insights, Instantly

Turn Raw Data into Actionable Insights

Leverage our Gemini-powered AI to perform comprehensive analysis of whole slide images. It identifies cellular morphologies, quantifies biomarkers, and highlights areas of interest with incredible speed and accuracy.

  • Detect & Classify Malignancies: Identify cancerous cells and suggest diagnoses with high confidence scores.
  • Automated Quantification: Automatically count mitotic figures and quantify biomarker expression (e.g., Ki-67, PD-L1).
  • Interactive Heatmaps: Visualize areas of concern on the whole slide image to guide your review process efficiently.
Collaborate with Confidence

A Platform Built for Teamwork

Seamlessly request second opinions, share specific annotations, and hold real-time case discussions. A complete audit trail ensures every action is tracked for compliance and quality assurance.

  • Instant Consultations: Request a second opinion from a colleague with a single click, directing the case to their queue.
  • Shared Workspace: All annotations and comments are synced in real-time for transparent collaboration.
  • Centralized Dashboard: Manage your case queue, prioritize STAT cases, and track every case from accession to sign-out.
Accelerate Your Research

Build Complex Data Cohorts in Seconds

Move beyond individual cases. Utilize our natural language query engine to analyze model performance, compare findings across patient histories, and uncover trends that drive the next generation of medical breakthroughs.

  • Natural Language Queries: Describe the cohort you need in plain English and let the AI build the dataset for you.
  • Longitudinal Insights: Track disease progression and treatment response across a patient's entire case history.
  • Federated Learning: Participate in multi-institutional studies to train more robust models without sharing sensitive patient data.
Secure, Scalable, and Integrated

Enterprise-Grade Architecture

Manage your entire organization with ease. Our platform features granular role-based access control, real-time monitoring of LIS/EHR integrations, and a HIPAA-compliant architecture.

  • Role-Based Access Control: Define custom roles and permissions to ensure users only see the data they need.
  • Integration Monitoring: A centralized dashboard for administrators to monitor the health of all system integrations.
  • HIPAA-Compliant: Built from the ground up with security and patient privacy as our top priority.
